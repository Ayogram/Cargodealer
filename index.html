Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our shipping and logistics services. For anything else, reach out on WhatsApp.

How do I track my shipment? +
You can easily track your shipment by entering your 6-digit Tracking ID (e.g. CDI-123456) in the Track Shipment box on our homepage.
How long does Air Freight take to arrive? +
Air Freight shipments typically arrive within 3-5 business days depending on the origin and destination hubs. Customs clearance may add an additional 1-2 days.
Do you offer door-to-door delivery? +
Yes! Our courier and transportation services ensure that your package can be picked up from your address and delivered directly to the recipient's door safely and securely.
